Dear People of God.

Based on the recommendations of the CDC, our national leaders and our Bishop; The Vestry has decided to suspend all services, meetings and outreach programs of St. John’s congregation. In the coming days and weeks Mother Sally and I will be sending pertinent information as well as articles, links to prayers, meditations and church services to aid you in your spiritual journey during this difficult time.

  • Attached is the text of the letter from Bishop Owensby. Will be sent separately.
  • Here is a link to an absolute gold mine of useful material: https://www.missionstclare.com/english/ It contains the proper daily offices (Morning, Noonday, and Evening prayer) complete for each day with optional recorded hymns and canticles. Don’t overlook the many items in the list of contents including hymns.
  • St. Marks Cathedral Seattle: https://saintmarks.org/worship/live-stream/ A great number of recorded services.

Mother Sally welcomes your calls especially for pastoral matters and Conrad will be in the office for a time each day so call or send an email if we can be of service to you.
